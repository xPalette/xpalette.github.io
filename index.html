xPalette does not collect or share any data when being used.

When using the application, this is how your data is stored:

Your artwork, brushes and color palettes are all saved directly into your iPad.

You are not required to login to access your artworks.

xPalette do not connect to any extern web services at any time.

When using image import, xPalette will request access your camera. xPalette does not collect or store your camera data at any time.

We believes strongly in not collecting or sharing your data, period. It belongs to you.
